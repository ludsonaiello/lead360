Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Lead360 ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information with care and respect. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our platform and services (collectively, the "Services").

By accessing or using Lead360, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our Services.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide to Us

We collect information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, business name, and business address when you create an account.
  • Business Data: Information about your leads, customers, quotes, projects, invoices, and other business operations you enter into the platform.
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when you contact our support team or communicate with us.
  • Payment Information: Billing information and payment card details (processed securely through third-party payment processors).

2.2 Google Calendar Integration

When you choose to connect your Google Calendar to Lead360, we collect and access the following calendar information with your explicit permission:

  • Calendar Events: We read your calendar events to check availability and prevent double-bookings when scheduling appointments.
  • Event Creation: We create calendar events when appointments are booked through our AI assistant or manual scheduling tools.
  • Event Updates: We update calendar events when appointments are rescheduled or modified within Lead360.
  • Event Deletion: We delete calendar events when appointments are cancelled in the system.

How we use your calendar data:

  • Schedule customer appointments automatically through our AI phone assistant
  • Send appointment reminders to customers via SMS or email
  • Coordinate team schedules and resource allocation
  • Sync project milestones, site visits, and deadlines
  • Prevent scheduling conflicts and double-bookings

Important: We only access the calendars you explicitly authorize. Your calendar data is never sold, shared with third parties for marketing purposes, or used for any purpose other than providing scheduling functionality within Lead360. You can revoke calendar access at any time through your Google Account settings or by disconnecting the integration within Lead360.

2.3 Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we automatically collect certain information, including:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our Services, including features used, pages viewed, and actions taken.
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and other technical information.
  • Cookies and Similar Technologies: We use cookies, web beacons, and similar tracking technologies to collect information and improve your experience.
  • Log Data: Server logs that include IP addresses, access times, and pages viewed.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Service Delivery: To provide, maintain, and improve our Services, including customer support.
  • Calendar Management: To schedule appointments, send reminders, coordinate team schedules, and manage project timelines when you connect your Google Calendar.
  • Account Management: To create and manage your account, process transactions, and send service-related communications.
  • Communication: To send you updates, newsletters, marketing materials, and other information that may interest you (you can opt out at any time).
  • Analytics and Improvement: To analyze usage patterns, improve our Services, and develop new features.
  • Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests from public authorities.

Specific Use of Google Calendar Data: Calendar information is used exclusively within Lead360 to provide appointment scheduling, reminder notifications, and calendar synchronization features. We do not use calendar data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose unrelated to scheduling functionality.

4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., hosting, payment processing, analytics). These providers are contractually obligated to protect your information.
  • Google API Services: When you connect your Google Calendar, we access Google's APIs to read and write calendar data on your behalf. This data is transmitted securely between Lead360 and Google's servers and is subject to both our Privacy Policy and Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Business Transfers: If we are involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government regulation, or to protect our rights and safety.
  • With Your Consent: We may share information with third parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so.

Google Calendar Data Sharing: We do not share your Google Calendar data with any third parties except as necessary to provide the calendar integration service (e.g., communication with Google's servers). Calendar data is never sold, used for advertising, or shared for marketing purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest using SSL/TLS protocols
  • Secure authentication and access controls
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-tenant data isolation to ensure your business data remains separate from other users

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies and similar tracking technologies to enhance your experience and collect usage data. Cookies are small data files stored on your device.

Types of Cookies We Use:

  • Essential Cookies: Required for the Services to function properly (e.g., session management, authentication).
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our Services and identify areas for improvement.
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and preferences.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect the functionality of our Services.

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this Privacy Policy. When you close your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information within a reasonable timeframe, unless we are required to retain it for legal or regulatory purposes.

Google Calendar Data Retention: When you disconnect your Google Calendar integration or close your account, we immediately stop accessing your calendar data and delete any cached calendar information from our servers within 30 days. You can also revoke Lead360's access to your Google Calendar at any time through your Google Account permissions page at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

8. Your Rights and Choices

You have certain rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: You can request access to the personal information we hold about you.
  • Correction: You can request that we correct inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: You can request that we delete your personal information, subject to certain exceptions.
  • Opt-Out: You can opt out of marketing communications by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails.
  • Data Portability: You can request a copy of your data in a structured, commonly used format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at privacy@lead360.app.

Managing Google Calendar Permissions

You have full control over Lead360's access to your Google Calendar:

  • Disconnect Integration: You can disconnect the Google Calendar integration at any time from your Lead360 account settings.
  • Revoke Access: You can revoke Lead360's access to your Google Calendar through your Google Account at https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.
  • Delete Data: When you disconnect the integration, we will delete all cached calendar data within 30 days.
  • Review Permissions: You can review and modify the specific calendar permissions granted to Lead360 at any time.

9. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have additional rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA):

  • The right to know what personal information we collect, use, and disclose
  • The right to request deletion of your personal information
  • The right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • The right to non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

To exercise your CCPA rights, please contact us at privacy@lead360.app.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

11. Third-Party Services and Integrations

Lead360 integrates with third-party services to enhance functionality. When you connect these services, you authorize us to access and use data from those platforms as described in this Privacy Policy.

Google Calendar Integration

Lead360's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically:

  • We only request calendar access permissions necessary for scheduling features
  • Calendar data is not used for serving advertisements
  • Calendar data is not sold to third parties
  • Calendar data is not used for creditworthiness determination or lending purposes
  • Human access to calendar data is restricted to authorized personnel for support and debugging purposes only

Our Google Calendar integration allows you to:

  • View your calendar availability within Lead360
  • Create appointments automatically through our AI assistant
  • Sync project milestones and deadlines to your calendar
  • Send calendar invitations to customers and team members

You can disconnect the Google Calendar integration at any time from your Lead360 account settings or by revoking access through your Google Account.

12. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your country of residence. We take appropriate measures to ensure that your information receives an adequate level of protection in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

13. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy on our website and updating the "Effective Date" at the top. Your continued use of our Services after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

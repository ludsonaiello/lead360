What is Lead360?

Lead360 is a complete CRM and ERP platform built specifically for construction and service businesses. We replace scattered tools—spreadsheets, sticky notes, QuickBooks, email threads—with one unified system that runs your entire operation.

Why Lead360 Exists

Traditional CRM systems (Salesforce, HubSpot) were built for sales teams—not contractors. Accounting software (QuickBooks, FreshBooks) handles finances but can't manage leads or projects. Construction-specific tools are often too complex or too narrow in scope.

Lead360 bridges this gap. We built a single platform that handles everything from the moment a lead calls you to the day you collect final payment—specifically designed for how construction and service businesses actually operate.

Data Privacy & Security

We only access calendar data you explicitly grant. We request read and write access to manage appointments on your behalf.

Your calendar data is encrypted in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption protocols.

We never sell or share your data with third parties. Calendar information is used solely to provide scheduling services within Lead360.

You can revoke access anytime from your Google Account settings or by disconnecting the integration in Lead360.

For complete details, review our Privacy Policy and Terms of Service.

Lead360's use of information received from Google APIs adheres to Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.
